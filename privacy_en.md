# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: February 3, 2026_

Plannio respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect,
use, and protect your information when you use the Plannio mobile application.

Plannio is a mobile application designed to help friends and families plan
gatherings, create shared lists, and track expenses in a simple and organized
way.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

Plannio collects only the information necessary to provide its core features.

- **User-provided information:**  
  Organization names, descriptions, dates, list items, expense details,
  participant names, and optional notes entered by users.

- **Technical information:**  
  App version, device type, and anonymous usage statistics collected in
  aggregated form.

Plannio does **not** collect sensitive personal data such as identity numbers,
payment card details, or precise location data.  
Information like phone numbers or locations is stored only if explicitly
entered by the user.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used to:

- Create and manage organizations and shared lists  
- Enable collaboration between participants  
- Improve app performance and user experience  
- Ensure security and prevent misuse  
- Comply with legal obligations  

---

## 3. Legal Basis

Personal data is processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws,
based on:

- Performance of the service  
- Legal obligations  
- Legitimate interests (such as improving and securing the app)  
- User consent, where required  

---

## 4. Technologies Used

Plannio may use anonymous usage data to improve functionality and performance.
This data is not used to personally identify users.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

Your data is:

- **Not sold or rented** to third parties  
- Shared only when necessary to provide the service (e.g. hosting or technical
  infrastructure providers)  
- Shared with authorities only when legally required  

All third parties process data in accordance with confidentiality obligations.

---

## 6. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes
described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
Upon request, data may be deleted, anonymized, or restricted.

---

## 7. Data Security

Plannio applies appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect
data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.  
However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

---

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to:

- Access your personal data  
- Request correction or deletion  
- Object to data processing  
- Request restriction of processing  

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted
on this page and will take effect immediately upon publication.
