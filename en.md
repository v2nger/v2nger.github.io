# Plannio

**Manage group shopping costs with ease.**

Plannio is a mobile application that helps groups
fairly and clearly split the cost of a shared shopping,
even when one person completes the purchase.

Collect orders, do the shopping,
enter the receipt ‚Äî Plannio calculates the per-person cost.

---

## How does it work?

1. Create an organization and let participants submit their orders  
2. The organizer completes the shopping and enters the receipt details  
3. Plannio automatically calculates the per-person settlement  

All participants can review the calculation
and confirm their payable or receivable amounts.

---

## Why Plannio?

In group shopping,
the real challenge is often not the shopping itself,
but settling the costs afterward.

Plannio helps you:

- Fairly distribute shared expenses  
- Keep calculations transparent for everyone  
- Track payments in an organized way  
- Reduce confusion within the group  

A simple, clear, and reliable solution.

---

## Smart support (Premium)

Plannio Premium analyzes orders and expenses
to help create more balanced payment scenarios.

Premium features include:

- AI-powered order and expense analysis  
- Alternative fair-splitting scenarios

- PDF export and sharing options  

---

## Who is it for?

- Families and groups of friends  
- Any group where one person handles the shopping  
- Users who want clear and fair expense sharing  

Plannio adapts to various group spending scenarios.
