# Plannio

**Plan together. Without the chaos.**

Plannio is a mobile app designed to help friends and families
plan gatherings, create shared lists, and track expenses
in a clear and simple way.

---

## What does it do?

- Create shared lists for any plan  
- Assign items to participants  
- Track progress and expenses transparently
